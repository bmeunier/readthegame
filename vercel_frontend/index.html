This is version 3 of a weird little thing I’ve been building in public.

Version 1 was just me dumping raw podcast transcripts into a chatbot. It didn’t work. At all.

Version 2 was better. It let you search and grab clips fast. But the whole thing was still kinda messy and hard to follow.

Now with version 3, I’m starting fresh. Instead of jumping straight to search, I wanted to make each episode easy to read and listen to at the same time. Once that's done, I feed everything into a third-party RAG and search system to help pull out what really matters.
